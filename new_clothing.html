    Clothing

    In traditional Balinese weddings, the bride and groom will wear vibrant, elaborate attire.

    The Payas Agung headpieace is an important piece. The height of the headpiece tends to increase with the status of the family.

    Guests will often wear kebaya or batik to weddings.

    Typically guests can wear any color. However, black is usually avoided.
