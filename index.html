    When & Where

    Balinese Traditional Wedding

    This ceremony is the culmination of a week-long sequence of rituals and prayers. The main ceremony will take place in the morning, but guests are welcome to stop by at any time of the day. The ceremony will be held at Ariya's family home in Tabanan regency. All are welcome.

    This is a more intimate, half-day event. It will be a more modern wedding ceremony involving a renewal of vows followed by a dinner reception. However, due to venue constraints, attendance is by invite only and is limited to close family and friends.

    Questions



    Will I receive a physical invite?

    Hopefully, yes! If you live abroad it might take longer for an invite to appear in the mail. If your address has changed, please email us with your new address so we can send an invite to the correct one. You will also receive a digital invite sent to either your email, social media and/or messaging apps (like WhatsApp, Telegram, LINE).



    I want to attend the traditional Balinese ceremony, what is the dress code?

    There is no set dress code! Please wear what is comfortable for you -- just don't wear black (black is for funerals). The majority of people attending will be wearing traditional batik or kebaya. So, if you would like to blend in more, feel free to wear these traditional garments! It is completely okay to wear these no matter your nationality or ethnic background.



    Do you have a wedding registry?

    Not exactly. We do appreciate any gifts, but we definitely do not require it. Especially since this is a destination wedding, and many of our guests will be flying from out of town, we are not expecting gifts.



    There are two events, which should I attend?

    The first event is a traditional Balinese wedding ceremony and is open to all our invites. The second is a more intimate wedding ceremony that is invite only. If you received an invite to the second event and only have time for one we highly recommend prioritising the second one.



    I have dietary restrictions, what kind of food will be there?

    We are planning to provide a wide range of food options ranging from: pork, beef, poultry, fish, vegan, spicy and non-spicy food. If you have specific dietary restrictions, please email us and let us know as early as possible! We will do our best to accommodate it as best we can.



    I'm flying in to Bali, what area should I consider for accomodation?

    You can stay wherever you want, really! The traditional wedding ceremonies and rituals will take place at Ariya's grandparents' house in Tabanan regency. However, since it is more difficult to get to, we are planning to charter several minibuses departing from the Denpasar area for guests. The second wedding celebration will also be held in South Kuta. If you are concerned about having easier transportation, we recommend staying in the Denpasar area.
