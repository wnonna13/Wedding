I won't be able to make it, will you livestream it?


No worries! We are planning on livestreaming the ceremony on Zoom. If you can't make it to the venue physically, we would love to have you virtually! Please RSVP and choose the "virtual" link and we will make sure to get back with you with the Zoom link once we have finalized it!

However, due to the venue limits, physical attendance at the second event is limited to close friends and family only. Both events will be livestreamed and anyone is able to join virtually regardless.
