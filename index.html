Our Story

It all started with a conversation on Bumble and a movie date to watch Little Women. Two weeks later the Covid pandemic hit and we began substituting in-person dates with virtual ones.


Three years passed by, and despite multiple instances of long-distance -- Wynonna with her teaching and Ariya with his journalism -- our bond has only grown stronger.


Now, we hope to make our bond official and permanent. We hope to mark this occasion with all our loved ones and we look forward to celebrating with you all!
