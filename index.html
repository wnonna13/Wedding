Our Story

It all started two weeks before Covid hit, with a conversation on Bumble and a movie date to watch "Little Women".

Three years and multiple long-distance segments later, our bond has only grown stronger.

Now, we hope to make this bond permanent and official and we'd love to share it with all our loved ones. We look forward to celebrating and partying with you all!

When & Where

July 28th 2024

Balinese Traditional Wedding

This ceremony is the culmination of a week-long set of rituals and prayers. The main ceremony will take place in the morning, but guests are welcome to stop by at any time of the day. The location is Ariya's family home in Tabanan. All are welcome to attend this traditional ceremony.

July 30th 2024

Intimate Ceremony & Dinner

This is a more intimate, half-day event. It will be a more modern wedding ceremony involving a renewal of vows and followed by a dinner reception. However, due to the venue constraints, attendance is by invite only and is limited to close family and friends.

Questions

I can't make it to either event, will you livestream it?

Yes, we are planning on livestreaming parts of the events on Youtube. So, if you can't make it to the venue physically, we would love to have you virtually! Please RSVP and let us know you would like a link.



Will I receive a physical invite?

Hopefully, yes! If you live abroad it might take longer for an invite to appear in the mail. If your address has changed, please email us and let us know so we can send one to the right address.



I want to attend the traditional Balinese ceremony, what is the dress code?

There is no set dress code! Please wear what is comfortable for you. The majority of people attending will be wearing traditional batik or kebaya. So, if you would like to 'match' more, feel free to wear batik or kebaya!



There are two events, which should I attend?

The first event is a traditional Balinese wedding ceremony and is open to all our invites. The second is a more intimate wedding ceremony. However, due to the venue limits, physical attendance at the second event is limited to close friends and family only. Both events will be livestreamed and anyone is able to join virtually regardless.



I have dietary restrictions, what kind of food will be there?

We are hoping to provide a range of food for both the first traditional ceremony and the second, more intimate celebration. If you have specific dietary restrictions, please email us and let us know as early as possible! We will do our best to keep it in mind and make accomodations if necessary.
