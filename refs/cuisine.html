    Cuisine

    Below are some food and drinks you may encounter in Bali!
    Choose an option below to find out more. Some common allergens will be specified as well.

    Ayam Betutu, Bebek Betutu
    (Chicken Betutu, Duck Betutu)

    Steamed or Roasted duck or chicken is smothered in a mix of ginger, turmeric, peanuts and shrimp paste.

    Allergens: Shrimp, Peanuts, Coconut

    Nasi Campur Babi Guling
    (Suckling Pig Rice)

    This meal is made up of many different components including pork crackers, pork satay and spices. If you enjoy eating pork, don't miss out on this!

    Allergens: Coconut

    Ikan Bakar Jimbaran
    (Jimbaran-Style Grilled Fish)

    Named after the Jimbaran coastline, the fish is grilled and charred before brushed with a mixture of sweet soy sauce, garlic and turmeric.
    Allergens: Soybeans

    Urap Sayur Bali
    (Balinese Salad)

    Green beans, bean sprouts and various other vegetables mixed with coconut shavings.
    Allergens: Coconut

    Es Daluman
    (Grass Jelly Drink)

    A refreshing drink consisting of grass jelly pieces, palm sugar and coconut milk.
    Allergens: Coconut

    Klepon
    (Sweet Rice Cakes)

    Glutinous rice flour stuffed with palm sugar and often coated with coconut flakes. When you bite into it, the sugar oozes out!
    Allergens: Coconut
