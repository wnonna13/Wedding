    Regions

    A little spotlight on three of Bali's regions

    Tabanan

    Ariya's home regency, the traditional Balinese ceremony will take place here on July 25th. The largest refency in Bali, Tabanan is famous for Tanah Lot temple and the Jatiluwih rice terraces.

    Denpasar

    The capital city of Bali and the administrative heart of Bali. Boasting Ngurah Rai international airport, Denpasar is often the first point of entrance for many visitors.

    Kuta District

    One of the most populous tourist hubs in Bali. Home to popular destinations such as Legian and Seminyak beach. The modern, intimate wedding celebration occuring on July 27th will take place in South Kuta, not far Kuta district.
