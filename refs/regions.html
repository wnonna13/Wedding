    Regions

    There are many regions in Bali, you can read some of them below.

    Mesedek

    For this first step, the groom's family will visit the bride's home to ask permission from the bride and her family.

    Allergens: Shrimp, Peanuts, Coconut

    Dewasa Ayu

    After getting permission from the bride's family, the groom and his family will visit the elders to find an auspicious day (according to the Balinese calendar) to hold the wedding.

    Allergens: Coconut

    Memadik

    Traditionally, 3 days before the chosen wedding day, the groom will travel to the bride's family home and bring her to his own home. At the groom's home, rituals, ceremonies and discussions will take place between the couple, the family and the elders.
    Allergens: Soybeans

    Mesegeh Agung

    This is one of the many ceremonies that take place at the groom's home after Memadik. It is a ceremonial procession meant to welcome the bride into the family.
    Allergens: Coconut

    Mesakapan

    A refreshing drink consisting of grass jelly pieces, palm sugar and coconut milk.
    Allergens: Coconut

    Mekala-kalaan

    Glutinous rice flour stuffed with palm sugar and often coated with coconut flakes. When you bite into it, the sugar oozes out!
    Allergens: Coconut

    Mewidhi Widana

    Mewidhi Widana

    For this first step, the groom's family will visit the bride's home to ask permission from the bride and her family.

    Allergens: Shrimp, Peanuts, Coconut

    Ma Pejati

    The bride is bathed with a special mixture of flowers, turmeric and refined rice. It is to symbolize the change the bride will go from being a single woman to a married woman.

    Allergens: Coconut

    Mejauman

    During this ritual, the bride and groom will burn three bamboo sticks. It is believed that by burning the bamboo, the couple will be protected against malicious spirits that seek to disrupt the marriage.
    Allergens: Soybeans
